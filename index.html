Health & Recovery

Know how recovered you are — every morning.

GenieMax turns the data from your Bluetooth heart-rate strap into clear daily guidance: recovery, strain, and sleep — measured against your own baseline, kept private on your device.

What it does

Six things, one calm dashboard.

💚

Recovery score

A daily 0–100 read from your overnight HRV, resting heart rate, and sleep — compared to your own rolling baseline, not anyone else's.

🔥

Strain & load

See how much a day or a workout taxed you, with fitness, fatigue, and form trends so you train hard on the right days.

🌙

Sleep analysis

Stages, efficiency, regularity, and a personal sleep-need target — plus a plain-language read of what last night meant.

❤️

Live workouts

Start a session and watch heart-rate zones, calories, distance, and laps update in real time.

🧍

Body composition

Log scan results and track muscle, fat, and water over time with clear visual trends.

🤖

AI coach (BYOK)

Bring your own AI provider key and ask questions about your data — grounded, personal answers, on your terms.

Private by design

Your health data is yours.

📱

On-device first

Your metrics live in protected storage on your iPhone. There is no server quietly collecting your health data.

🔐

End-to-end encrypted sync

If you turn on sync, everything is encrypted on your device with a recovery phrase only you hold — the backend only ever stores ciphertext.

🚫

No ads, no tracking

No third-party analytics, no advertising, nothing sold. Clear the data anytime in Settings.
