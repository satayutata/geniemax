GenieMax turns the data from a Bluetooth heart-rate strap into clear, daily guidance — how recovered you are, how hard you trained, and how well you slept — measured against **your own** baseline and kept **private on your device**.

## Features

- **Recovery score** — daily 0–100 from overnight HRV, resting heart rate, and sleep vs. your rolling baseline.
- **Strain & training load** — day/workout load with fitness, fatigue, and form trends.
- **Sleep analysis** — stages, efficiency, regularity, and a personal sleep-need target.
- **Live workouts** — real-time heart-rate zones, calories, distance, and laps.
- **Body composition** — track muscle, fat, and water over time.
- **AI coach (bring your own key)** — grounded, personal answers about your data.

## Privacy

Your health data lives in protected storage on your iPhone. There is no server quietly collecting it. Optional features (encrypted sync, iCloud export, the AI coach, Apple Health) are **off by default** and only act when you turn them on. No ads, no third-party tracking, nothing sold. See the [Privacy Policy](https://satayutata.github.io/geniemax/privacy.html).

## Install

GenieMax is in beta on TestFlight: **[testflight.apple.com/join/XNHDWMgh](https://testflight.apple.com/join/XNHDWMgh)**

1. Install the free TestFlight app from the App Store.
2. Open the link above and tap **Install**.
3. Connect a compatible Bluetooth heart-rate strap and you're set.

## Requirements

iPhone on iOS 16+ · a compatible Bluetooth heart-rate strap · no subscription · bring your own AI provider key for the coach.
